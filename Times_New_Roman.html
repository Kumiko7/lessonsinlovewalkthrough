Walkthrough / Choices

Optional Happy Event: Choose "Check the kitchen" -> "Use Ami's computer". Enter password: "mom".
Phase 1: Essential Tools & Early Puzzles
  • Get the Phone: Go to "Live happily" → "Microwave". Enter code: 1853818235819. Select "Take your phone".
  • Make Early Calls: Use Phone → "Make a call":
    • Call 81319744253 (Sets up Red Key).
    • Call 81269154491 (Unlocks Mysterious Key / Key 9 immediately).
    • Call 81869913536 (Sets up Roux delivery).
  • Coffee Puzzle (Key 3 & Roach): Go to "Live happily" → "Coffee maker". Choose:
    1. French press
    2. Kalita Wave
    3. Phin
    4. AeroPress
    5. Chemex brewer
    This grants Spectacular Key (Key 3) and the Roach.
  • The Rabbit Doll (Key 2): Go to "Live happily" → "Rabbit doll". Click it 8 times total to get Cereal Key (Key 2).
Phase 2: The Hot Pot Components (Day Management)

Note: Use "Go to sleep" to advance days.

  • Day 2: "Live happily" → "Cabinet (Bottom left)" to get Parrot the Parrot.
  • Day 3: "Live happily" → "Floor mat". Select Lionfish (Crucial for Hot Pot).
  • Day 4: "Live happily" → "Trash can". Choose:
    1. Say hello to Frankie
    2. Remove your tongue
    3. Run in circles seven times
    4. Stare up at the sky
    5. Your hot sauce is delicious
    6. Spin
    7. Taking a drink
    This grants Pegasus Key (Key 1).
  • Day 5 (Computer Orders): Use Computer. Password: baby.
    • Select "Order mystery box 1" (For the Gun).
    • Select "Order mystery box 2". Enter code: 46782 (or 99635 / 392354 depending on your RNG).
Phase 3: Final Collection & Preparation
  • Advance to Day 1: Go to the Door → "Look down". Collect all deliveries: Red Key (Key 7), Roux, Mystery Box 1 (Gun), and Himawari's Key (Key 8).
  • The Fridge (IMPORTANT): "Live happily" → "Refrigerator". Select "Take the shampoo" then LEAVE IT ALONE on the second prompt. WARNING: If you have the Shampoo in your inventory during the final Dorms scene, you will fail the mission.
  • The Vent: Go to the Door → "Check the vent". Select "Continue gazing" 20 times to get the Centipede.
  • Cabinet Right: "Live happily" → "Cabinet (Bottom right)". Enter code: 231312423124 to get Squirrel the Dog.
  • Freezer: "Live happily" → "Freezer". Select "Insert roach" to get the Melon.
  • Schedule: "Live happily" → "Schedule" to get Flower Key (Key 4).
  • Romance (Key 5): "Kitchen" → "Fall in love". Choose:
    1. Be understanding
    2. Italian restaurant
    3. The Notebook
    4. No
    This grants Barbara's Key (Key 5).
Phase 4: The Escape
  • Cook Hot Pot (Key 6): "Kitchen" → "Hot pot". Add all 7 ingredients: Roux, Watermelon, Parrot, Squirrel, Fridge stuff, Centipede, and Lionfish. Then select "Finish the hot pot" to get Delicious Key (Key 6).
  • Unlock Lockers: Go to the Door → "Check the lockers". Use all 9 Keys to acquire Piece 1 through Piece 9.
  • Exit: Go to the Door. Ensure you have the Gun and all 9 Pieces. Select "Open the door" → "Leave the room".
Phase 5: The Mission (ResetSix2)

Visit every location listed in the menu and click through. When you reach Dorms:

  • If you followed the warning and DO NOT have the Shampoo, the "papercopsdone" flag will trigger.
  • Once all 18 locations are "cleared" (Gunpoint == 18), select "Complete Mission" at the bottom.
  • Select "Kill Ayane. Complete Your Mission." to reach the renpy.end_replay() and conclude the event.
