Chapter 1 1st Happy Loop

Category: Unindexed Events

Prerequisites

  • None

Next Events

  • Reset
  • Everything is Connected

Walkthrough / Choices

Requirement: To finish this event and trigger renpy.end_replay(), you must meet two conditions: god_love >= 5 and day > 6 (specifically Day 7).
Optional Happy Event: Go to the Soccer field to see the missable Happy Event V "Everything is Connected".
Step 1 (Increasing God Love): In the first menu, select "Go somewhere" -> "Koi Cafe" (or any location). In the second menu, you must select "Shrine". This increases your god_love stat by 1.
Step 2 (Advancing the Day): In the third menu, select "Go somewhere" -> "Bar" (or any location). This will trigger the day advancement logic and return you to the start of the loop.
Step 3 (The Grind): Repeat the sequence above until you have visited the Shrine at least 5 times.
Step 4 (Timing): Once you have 5 God Love, continue the loop until the game reaches Day 7 (Sunday). The game cycles from Day 1 to Day 7.
Step 5 (Final Choice): When god_love >= 5 and day == 7, a new option will appear in the first menu. Select "Go somewhere" -> "School" to reach the ending and exit the replay.
