SENSEI-QUEST

Category: Chapter 4

Prerequisites

  • Kittens (Part of event chain)

Next Events

  • Restart

Walkthrough / Choices

To successfully complete this section and execute renpy.end_replay(), you must obtain all four element orbs. To complete the combat sequences, you must avoid taking more than 30 points of damage in any individual fight (meaning senseidamage must remain 30 or lower).

Step 1: Resource Gathering (Currency Farming)
You require premium currency (pcur) to purchase maps, active skills, and equipment.

  • Select Fields of Despair and farm the Common Snail by selecting Attack -> Tackle. Each victory awards 5 pcur. Repeat this until you accumulate at least 500 pcur.
  • Return to the hub and enter Traveler’s Tavern. Buy the Fire Ball skill (500 pcur).
  • Return to the Fields of Despair. Farm the Metal Dogslime using Attack -> Fire Ball. Each victory awards 100 pcur. Repeat this until you have at least 4,900 pcur (or 8,900 pcur if you plan to unlock the optional Developer Island branch).

Step 2: Purchase Checklist
Travel to the shops to acquire the necessary keys, skills, and gear:

  • Traveler's Tavern: Purchase Quest: Sea of Stairs (100 pcur), Quest: Bedlehem (150 pcur), Quest: Unholy Cathedral (300 pcur), Venom Spit (50 pcur), Banana Blast (100 pcur), Bubble Beam (200 pcur), Sanitize (100 pcur), Fertilize (150 pcur), Salt Spray (50 pcur), and finally Quest: Palace of the Fist (250 pcur) which automatically redirects you back to the main hub.
  • Roadside Merchant: Purchase Sword of Hope (500 pcur), Shield of Resistance (600 pcur), Boots of Forgiveness (700 pcur), Steel Bucket (800 pcur), and Massage Oil (350 pcur).

Step 3: Solve Combat Puzzles to Earn the Orbs
Enter each quest zone from the main hub and input the specific choice sequence:

  • Sea of Stairs (Plant Orb):
    1. Choose Guard (Increases netkillpts to 1; 0 damage taken)
    2. Choose Attack -> Use...Massage Oil? (Increases netkillpts to 2; 10 damage taken)
    3. Choose Attack -> Fire Ball (Increases netkillpts to 3; 20 damage taken)
    4. Choose Attack -> Tackle (Obtains plantorb; exits to hub)
  • Bedlehem (Worm Orb):
    1. Choose Attack -> Venom Spit (Increases mannykillpts to 1; 10 damage taken)
    2. Choose Attack -> Salt Spray (Increases mannykillpts to 2; 20 damage taken)
    3. Choose Attack -> Bubble Beam (Increases mannykillpts to 3; 20 damage taken)
    4. Choose Guard (Obtains wormorb; exits to hub)
  • Palace of the Fist (Fist Orb):
    1. Choose Attack -> Banana Blast (Increases karatekillpts to 1; 10 damage taken)
    2. Choose Guard (Increases karatekillpts to 2; 20 damage taken)
    3. Choose Attack -> Sanitize (Increases karatekillpts to 3; 30 damage taken)
    4. Choose Attack -> Tackle (Obtains fistorb; exits to hub)
  • Unholy Cathedral (Red Orb):
    1. Choose Attack -> Use...Massage Oil? (Increases twinskillpts to 1; 10 damage taken)
    2. Choose Attack -> Fertilize (Increases twinskillpts to 2; 20 damage taken)
    3. Choose Guard (Increases twinskillpts to 3; 20 damage taken)
    4. Choose Attack -> Sanitize (Obtains redorb; exits to hub)

Step 4: Summon Final Boss
With all four orbs in your inventory, select Meowri’s House from the main hub, and choose "Summon the final boss". This triggers renpy.end_replay().

Step 5: Final Boss Combat (Orb Fight)
Once the final boss is summoned, you will engage in the Orb Fight combat sequence. You must defeat the boss while keeping senseidamage at or below 50. Use the following specific choice sequence:

  1. Choose Attack -> Venom Spit (Increases orbkillpts to 1)
  2. Choose Attack -> Bubble Beam (Increases orbkillpts to 2)
  3. Choose Attack -> Banana Blast (Increases orbkillpts to 3)
  4. Choose Guard (Increases orbkillpts to 4)
  5. Choose Attack -> Fire Ball (Increases orbkillpts to 5)
  6. Choose Attack -> Fertilize (Triggers endoffinalbosstime and completes the fight)

Optional Branch: Developer Island
There is an optional boss sequence that leads outside this file to postfreddeathscene:

  • Prerequisites: Buy Ancient Map Piece 1 from the Tavern (1000 pcur) and Ancient Map Piece 2 from the Roadside Merchant (3000 pcur). Make sure fredisdead is False.
  • Go to Developer Island to battle Fred (Max damage limit is 100):
    1. Attack -> Tackle (killpts: 1)
    2. Attack -> Use...Massage Oil? (killpts: 2)
    3. Attack -> Fire Ball (killpts: 3)
    4. Guard (killpts: 4)
    5. Attack -> Venom Spit (killpts: 5)
    6. Attack -> Sanitize (killpts: 6)
    7. Guard (killpts: 7)
    8. Attack -> Banana Blast (killpts: 8)
    9. Attack -> Fire Ball (killpts: 9)
    10. Guard (killpts: 10)
    11. Attack -> Tackle (Defeats Fred and jumps to postfreddeathscene)
