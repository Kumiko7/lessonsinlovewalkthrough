A Life of Prizes

Category: Chapter 2

Prerequisites

  • The Light of Last Summer (Part of event chain)

Next Events

  • Utinam Ne Illum Numquam Conspexissem

Walkthrough / Choices

This event acts as a meta-puzzle loop. To finish the event and reach the conclusion, you must collect 10 Keypoints to unlock the final escape option.
To see the Missable Happy Event "Lamb Legs", use the laptop and enter the lottery number: 157842 (requires having seen the happy event "Good Boy" or you will be flagged as cheater.
1. Initial Dialogue: When prompted, select "all I see is pain" to proceed to the room loop.
2. The Hub: You are now in a hub menu. Your goal is to enter the "ESCAPE!!!" sub-menu and solve the book puzzles, plus one additional task.
3. The Book Puzzles (8 Keypoints): Enter "ESCAPE!!!" and solve the following:
  • Book 1: Enter 85.
  • Book 2: Enter denim.
  • Book 3: Enter judas.
  • Book 4: Enter 18.
  • Book 5: This requires a specific sequence:
    • Select Book 5 (you will fail the first time, but it flags the item needed).
    • Go back to the Hub and select "Use communication device".
    • Enter the number 08023231234 to call Tsuneyo and get the hint.
    • Return to Book 5 and select Coffee.
  • Book 6: Enter marsh warbler.
  • Book 7: Enter 4863.
  • Book 8: Solve the multi-part quiz with these answers: 1788, green, 80, 4, 31, magpie, perception.
4. Book 9 (1 Keypoint): This book depends on your personality trait chosen during the Christmas event (Callous, Calm, or Concerned):
  • If Callous: Simply select the book to get the point.
  • If Calm: Go to "Eat Something" in the Hub. Eat Live Squid 10 times. Then return to Book 9.
  • If Concerned: Select Book 9 once. Go to the Hub and "Use the cool rectangle machine" (PC). Enter password Boobies123. Select "Unlock all puzzle answers" and switch your trait to Callous. Return to Book 9.
5. The Final Keypoint (1 Keypoint):
  • Go to "Eat Something" in the Hub.
  • Select White Rice and repeat this action 23 times. On the 23rd time, you will receive the final keypoint.
6. The Escape: Now that you have 10 keypoints, a new option will appear in the main hub: "ESCAPE (For Real)". Select it to finish the event.
